Simon"Ghost"Riley

Lieutenant Simon "Ghost" Riley is a British special forces operator, and a prominent member of Task Force 141, known for his iconic skull-patterned balaclava, headset, and dark red sunglasses.

Story

Simon Riley was born in Manchester, England and Simon saw the 9/11 attacks and joined the British military, and later became a member of the Special Air Service. He never really explains why he wears a mask all the time. Some says that "Ghost" is a nickname given to special operatives and when one "Ghost" dies, another one will take its place. This make it look like Ghost is immortal.

Military

Lieutenant Simon “Ghost” Riley was a British special forces operator that has appeared in multiple Call of Duty. Ghost uses his stealth to gain the tactical advantage on enemies, ensuring that if he's on the battlefield, no one is safe. He has proven himself to be a feared combatant on more than one occasion, effortlessly taking down entire compounds packed with enemies with brutal efficiency.
